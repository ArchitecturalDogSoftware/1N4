## How Jaxydog Handles Data

The primary instance of 1N4 is operated by her author, Jaxydog.
This section pertains to how she will handle data produced by 1N4,
instances hosted by third party may handle user data differently.

- Except in unusual circumstances required for moderation
  (such as reporting user content that violates laws or platform terms of service),
  Jaxydog will not share user-identifiable data.
  - Where data is shared, such as for debugging, it will be strictly anonymous.
  - Obviously, this does not apply to situations where sharing data is the feature,
    such as sharing the results of polls with other users.
- Discord may handle data from your interactions with 1N4 differently.
  See their privacy policy at <https://discord.com/privacy>.
